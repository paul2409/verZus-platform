# M9 — Eight controlled stages

1. **M9.1 — Crew foundation and approved responsive profile**
   - Crew identity, statistics, tabs, overview, roster preview, requests preview, activity, rankings, achievements and settings summary.
2. **M9.2 — No-crew state, discovery, search and filters**
   - Recommended Crews, eligibility, join intent, URL state and empty results.
3. **M9.3 — Crew creation and identity assets**
   - Name, tag, game, region, crest, banner, visibility, review and creation persistence.
4. **M9.4 — Schemas, APIs, adapters and query resources**
   - Independent profile, roster, requests, activity, rankings, achievements and settings resources.
5. **M9.5 — Invites, applications and membership**
   - Idempotent invite, application, join, accept, decline, leave and expiry flows.
6. **M9.6 — Roles, permissions and member management**
   - Owner, captain, manager, member and trial permissions; kick, promote, demote and transactional ownership transfer.
7. **M9.7 — Lifecycle, activity reliability and destructive operations**
   - Forming, active, inactive, suspended, disbanded and archived states; leave, disband, partial failure and audit controls.
8. **M9.8 — Testing, observability and release**
   - Unit, component, integration, E2E, accessibility, visual regression, telemetry, health, immutable artifact and rollback.
